#BLT
  An all American classic
  
    ### Ingredients 
    - 4 pieces of bacon
    - 1 slice of tomato
    - 2 leaves of lettuce
    - 2 slices of whole wheat bread
    
    ### Make it
    -cook the bacon
    -place bacon on bread along with tomato and lettuce
    -cover with other piece of bread
    -(optional) add mayo, mustard, or ketchup
